# Code of Conduct

This project welcomes focused, respectful collaboration from researchers,
engineers, and maintainers.

## Expected behavior

- Be specific, evidence-oriented, and kind in technical disagreement.
- Assume research claims need reproduction, tests, or source-backed rationale.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on the repository's code, docs,
  experiments, and maintenance workflows.
- Respect privacy, licensing boundaries, and embargoed or private data.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Posting secrets, private data, exploit payloads, or non-public model/data
  artifacts.
- Repeatedly derailing technical threads after maintainers redirect the topic.
- Misrepresenting experimental results, licenses, authorship, or provenance.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block users; or
take other proportionate action to protect the project and its contributors.
